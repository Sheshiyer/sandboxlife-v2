## ğŸš¶ User Journey: A Walkthrough

Follow these steps to master the Adventurer's path.

### Step 1: The Hub (Dashboard)
Upon entering, you see the **Quadrants Board**.
*   **Recent Cards**: Your last 4 entries float in 3D space within their respective quadrants.
*   **Interact**: Hover over a card to see details. Click to flip it and see your "Quest Log" (entry details).
*   **HUD**: The top bar shows your current **Mana**, **Level**, and **Title**.

### Step 2: Choosing a Quest
To write a new entry:
1.  Click the **Gold/Yellow Action Button (âš”ï¸)** in the bottom right corner.
2.  **The Choice Modal** appears with three paths:
    *   **ğŸ“œ Daily Quest**: Quick daily reflection.
    *   **ğŸ“– Campaign Tome**: Long-form book entry.
    *   **âœ¨ Oracle's Wisdom**: A short thought or status update.
3.  Select your path to travel to the **Scribe's Desk**.

### Step 3: The Scribe's Desk
You are now in the writing interface.
1.  **Select a Sigil**: Choose an icon that represents your current state or topic.
2.  **The Prompt**: A parchment reveals a question based on your chosen sigil.
3.  **Inscribe**: Write your answer in the text area.
4.  **Seal Entry**: Click "Seal Entry" to save. You will see a wax seal animation confirming your success and earning you XP.

### Step 4: The Map View
Want to see how far you've come?
1.  On the Dashboard, toggle the view from **"Board"** to **"Map"** in the top center HUD.
2.  **The Timeline**: Your entries appear as nodes on a winding path.
3.  **Scroll**: Journey back in time to review past quests.

### Step 5: The Party (Social)
Adventure is better with friends.
1.  Open the **Menu** and select **"Party Members"** (Friends).
2.  **Recruit**: Search for other users to add to your party.
3.  **Inspect**: View their profiles to see their **Level**, **Title**, and **Avatar**.
4.  **The Tavern**: Visit the **"Missives"** (Inbox) to chat in the global tavern or send private messages.
